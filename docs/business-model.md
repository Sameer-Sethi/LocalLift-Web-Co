## 2. Client Acquisition Channels

### Cold Outreach (60% of initial clients)
- **LinkedIn**: Target business owners without websites or with outdated sites
- **Email**: Personalized audit emails to businesses with poor websites
- **Volume**: 20-30 personalized outreaches/day
- **Expected conversion**: 3-5% response rate → 1-2% close rate

### Inbound (30% at scale)
- **SEO**: Rank for "website designer [city]", "business website cost", etc.
- **Content**: Blog posts, case studies, social proof
- **Social Media**: LinkedIn thought leadership, portfolio showcase

### Referrals (10% growing to 30%)
- **Referral bonus**: ₹5,000 credit or 10% commission per referred client
- **Post-project follow-up**: Ask happy clients for referrals at 30/60/90 days

## 4. Pricing Psychology

### Why Growth is Most Attractive
1. **Anchor effect**: Starter seems limited, Premium seems expensive → Growth feels "just right"
2. **Value stacking**: Growth includes features worth ₹80,000+ individually
3. **Social proof badge**: "Most Popular" creates herd mentality
4. **Visual prominence**: Larger card, highlighted border, scaled up

### Value Justification
- Average ROI from a properly built website: 5-10x the investment in Year 1
- If the website generates just 2 extra leads/month × ₹25,000 avg. deal = ₹6,00,000/year
- The website pays for itself in 1-2 months
